Pic-a-Place was founded with the aim of providing both domestic and international clients with a range of best-value travel products and services along with dedicated round-the-clock customer support. African Dew Tours & Travel is a registered member of Kenya Association of Travel Agents(KATA), Kenya Association of Tour Operators(KATO) and American Society of Travel Agents(ASTA).We are also fully accredited by International Air transport Association (I.A.T.A) who regulate our travel services.
